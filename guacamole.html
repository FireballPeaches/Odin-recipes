Guacamole

>

Guacamole! Did you know that over 2 billion pounds of avocados are consumed each year in the U.S.? That's over 7 pounds per person. I'm guessing that most of those avocados go into what has become America's favorite dip: guacamole.

Ingredients

  • Ripe avocados
  • Salt
  • Lime juice
  • Cilantro
  • Red onion
  • Tomato
  • Diced chilis

Making Guacamole

  1. Peel and chop the avocados into 1/2" pieces, lightly mash.
  2. Chop onion and tomato, quater lime, cut cilantro into small pieces
  3. Add onion, tomato, chile, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and peper. Stir to combine and taste.
  4. Adjust the lime, salt, and peper as needed.
  5. Serve immediately or cover tightly and refrigerate until ready to serve.
