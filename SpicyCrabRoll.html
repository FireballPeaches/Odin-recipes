Spicy Crab Roll

>

Spicy Crab Roll is a popular Japanese sushi roll – creamy, satisfying, and fiery spicy. The kani crab stick is shredded and then seasoned with sriracha spicy mayo, then wrapped in nori seaweed sheets and seasoned rice.

Ingredients

  • Kani crab meat
  • Sriracha Sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Nori (seaweed Sheets)
  • Sushi rice
  • Sushi vinegar
  • Sesame seeds
  • You’ll also need a sushi rolling bamboo mat for easy rolling.

How to Make Spicy Crab Roll

  1. Cook rice

    2. Once cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and let it cool down slightly. When it’s still warm, stir in the sushi vinegar (or the mixture of rice vinegar, sugar, and salt). Mix well.

  2. Make Spicy Crab Salad

    3. Simply shred the imitation crab (kanikama), and add it to a mixing bowl. Season with sriracha sauce and mayo. Mix well

  3. Make the Spicy Crab Roll

    4. Cut the nori sheet into two halves with kitchen scissors.
